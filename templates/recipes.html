Cooking vegan and especially healthy can seem troublesome at first, but don't worry.

We prepared for you several easy and healthy whole food vegan recipe ideas we're sure you'll like!

Breakfast recipes

Easy Oatmeal
  • 1 cup of oats
  • 1 banana
  • 1 medjool date
  • 2 tsp cinnamon

Method:

  1. Blend banana and date with some water and cinnamon in a blender.
  2. Cook oats in the blended liquid until done.
  3. Serve hot with your favorite oatmeal toppings.
Tofu Scramble
  • 1 firm tofu (~200g)
  • 1 medium onion
  • 1 medium red pepper
  • 1/2 medium zucchini
  • 1 tbsp nutritional yeast
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/2 tsp cumin
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric
  • 1/4 tsp chili powder

Method:

  1. Crumble tofu in a bowl and add the spices, mix well.
  2. Saute chopped vegetables in a frying pan on medium heat until tender.
  3. Add crumbled tofu and mix it with vegetables.
  4. Saute until tofu is warm and serve it with some toast or any sidings you like.

Lunch recipes

Baked Tofu
  • 1 extra firm tofu

Marinade:

  • 1 tbsp mustard
  • 1 tbsp tahini
  • 2 tsp soy sauce
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/2 tsp onion powder
  • 1/4 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper (optional)

Method:

  1. Press tofu to release as much water as possible and slice it into strips or slices.
  2. Put the tofu in the marinade. If you want tofu to soak as much marinade as it can, leave it in fridge overnight or bake it straight away.
  3. Place the tofu on a baking sheet and bake at 175Â°C for 25 minutes.
  4. Serve with fresh veggies or any other sidings you like.
Bean Burrito
  • 250g cooked red kidney beans
  • 1 red or white onion, chopped in slices
  • 2 medium bell peppers (mix colors if you want), chopped in slices
  • 2 tortillas
  • 100g cooked rices (optional)
  • 1 tbsp salsa
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp onion powder
  • 1/2 tsp chilli powder
  • 1/2 tsp cumin powder

Method:

  1. Saute the veggies in a frying pan on medium heat, add the spices and saute until onion becomes a bit transparent.
  2. Put veggies to the side of the frying pan and add beans to the other side.
  3. Add spices and salsa to the beans and saute for 3-5 minutes on medium heat.
  4. Place beans, veggies and rice on a tortilla to assemble the burrito.

Dinner recipes

Lentil Pasta Sauce
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1 medium onion, diced
  • 1 medium carrot, grated
  • 1/2 medium red bell pepper, chopped
  • 1/2 medium zucchini, chopped (optional)
  • 250g cooked lentils
  • 500ml tomato sauce

Method:

  1. Saute onion, red bell pepper, carrot and zucchini on medium-high heat for 5 minutes or less.
  2. Add lentils, garlic, tomato sauce and simmer together for 10 minutes on medium heat.
  3. Serve with your favourite pasta.
Chickpea - Lentil Mix up
  • 250g cooked chickpeas
  • 250g cooked lentils
  • 100g cooked green peas
  • 1 white onion, chopped
  • 1 tbsp vegetable stock (optional)
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp onion powder

Method:

  1. Add onion and chickpeas in frying pan with vegetable stock, saute for 5 minutes on medium heat.
  2. Put lentils in the pan and mix. Add the soy sauce and spices and saute until lentils start to crumble a bit.
  3. Add green peas and remove the frying pan from the stove after 1 minute.
  4. Serve it with rice, quinoa or any other siding you like.
