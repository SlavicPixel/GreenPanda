"How will I get enough protein on a vegan diet?"


A question many of us asked when we first transitioned to a plant based only diet.


Have no fear!

Silly jokes aside, every plant has all essential amino acids our body needs.


Truth is, not all plants have a high amount of protein.

We listed down below top 5 sources of delicious plant protein to help you on your journey to become a healthy and strong vegan!

Psst... You probably already eat and like all of them.

Oats

Besides being a great meal to start your morning, oats are filled with healthy micro and macro nutrients with a good amount of protein.
11.1g of protein per 100g

Beans

Probably our favourite plant protein source, can be so easily incorporated into a lot of recipes and are always so delicious.
7.4g of protein per 100g

Lentils

Great source of potassium and very good for your heart. Lentils come in various colors and will make sure you enjoy every bite of them.
9.1g of protein per 100g

Chickpeas

They have a weird smell and look funny, but are very good for your tummy. Don't forget, you can make hummus with them.
10.7g of protein per 100g

Tofu

While sometimes unsure for newcomers, tofu can easily be made delicious with few ingredients and is packed with protein.
16.63g of protein per 100g

We have recipes which include all of these amazing protein sources.
